# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**Customer:** Sports enthusiasts looking for available courts. They browse venues, check real-time availability, book slots, and receive confirmations via email/WhatsApp. Context: mobile-first, often on-the-go, need fast booking flow.

**Admin/Operator:** Venue staff managing daily operations. They confirm/reject bookings, handle manual bookings from offline channels (WhatsApp/call), and update payment statuses. Context: desktop-heavy, need quick actions, clear status visibility.

**Owner:** Business owners managing multiple venues. They configure courts, pricing, operating hours, view occupancy reports, and manage admin/operator access. Context: occasional use, need high-level insights.

## Product Purpose

Lapangin is a PWA sports court booking system that helps venues accept online bookings, reduce scheduling conflicts, and provide a simple dashboard for operations. It bridges the gap between traditional phone/WhatsApp booking and modern digital management.

Success looks like: venues can accept bookings 24/7 without manual coordination, customers can book in under 2 minutes, and owners have clear visibility into occupancy and revenue.

## Brand Personality

Clean, efficient, trustworthy. Sports-focused without being flashy. Professional but approachable. Mobile-first simplicity.

Voice: Clear, direct, action-oriented. Tone: Helpful, confident, no-nonsense.

## Anti-references

- Overly complex admin interfaces (like enterprise ERP systems)
- Flashy sports-themed designs that sacrifice usability
- Slow, multi-step booking flows
- Cluttered dashboards with too many metrics
- Apps that look like generic SaaS templates

## Design Principles

- **Speed over aesthetics:** Booking flow must be fast; every extra click costs conversions
- **Clarity first:** Status, availability, and actions must be immediately obvious
- **Mobile-first:** Every feature must work well on small screens
- **Progressive enhancement:** Core functionality works without JavaScript; enhanced with JS
- **Data integrity:** Prevent double-booking at all costs; never show stale availability

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA minimum
- Mobile touch targets ≥ 44x44px
- Color contrast ≥ 4.5:1 for text
- Support reduced motion preferences
- Clear error messages in Indonesian and English
- Keyboard navigable admin interface
