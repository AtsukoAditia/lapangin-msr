# PRODUCT.md — Lapangin

## What
Lapangin is a sports venue booking platform for Indonesia. Users browse available courts, book time slots, and pay — all mobile-first.

## Users
- **Customers** — Jakarta-based sports players (futsal, badminton, padel, tennis, basketball, minisoccer)
- **Venue Owners** — manage courts, schedules, pricing, reviews
- **Admins** — platform oversight, analytics, support

## Register
Product webapp. Responsive, PWA-capable.

## Brand Voice
- Friendly, casual, Indonesian-first
- Clear over clever — users book fast, don't read essays
- Sport energy without being loud
- Trustworthy — money transactions need confidence

## Anti-References
- ❌ Overly corporate SaaS dashboards
- ❌ Neon-heavy gaming aesthetics
- ❌ Stock photo-heavy landing pages
- ❌ "Boost your bookings!" copy
- ❌ Complex multi-step wizards (keep booking ≤ 3 steps)

## Key Flows
1. **Browse → Book → Pay** (customer, ≤3 taps)
2. **Manage courts/slots** (owner)
3. **Analytics dashboard** (admin/owner)

## Design Priorities
1. **Speed** — fast load, minimal clicks to book
2. **Clarity** — no ambiguity on price, availability, or status
3. **Mobile-first** — 80%+ traffic is mobile
4. **Trust** — clean UI, clear confirmation, receipt visibility
